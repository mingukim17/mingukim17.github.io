Research Areas

Guidance and Control

Advanced guidance laws for missiles and UAVs. This includes nonlinear guidance logic, impact angle control, and cooperative guidance for multiple vehicles.

  • Nonlinear Guidance Laws
  • Impact Angle/Time Control
  • Optimal Control Applications

State Estimation & Navigation

Robust state estimation techniques for aerospace systems using Kalman Filters and adaptive filtering methods.

  • Integrated Navigation Systems (INS/GPS)
  • Target Tracking & Estimation
  • Multi-sensor Data Fusion

UAV & Autonomous Systems

Development of autonomous algorithms for UAVs, including path planning, collision avoidance, and vision-based control.

  • Vision-based Landing/Recovery
  • Swarm Control
  • Path Planning
