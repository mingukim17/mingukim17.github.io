Positions for graduate students and undergraduate research students are available. Highly motivated students are welcome.

The research topics include, but not limited to, guidance, control, state estimation, and applications to aerospace systems and autonomous systems including UAVs, missiles, spacecraft, mobility robots, and wearable devices etc. Refer to Research for details.

If you are interested in, please feel free to contact Prof. Mingu Kim via e-mail with your relevant information as below.

  • Brief CV
  • Your research interests and related skills
  • Your objectives (including your aspirations after completing your graduate/undergraduate course)
  • Additional information on relevant experiences (personal/group projects, interships, or work experience, etc.)
